About

I've spent 18 years building things for people who are trying to change the world.

I'm James Bolden — most people call me Jimbo. I'm based in Philadelphia, recently married to Emily, and we have three kittens named after states of liberation (Moksha, Samadhi, and Nirvana — they're chaos incarnate).

I founded Iris Cocreative to serve consciousness-focused organizations, transformative communities, and purpose-driven businesses. My clients include the Holomovement, Source of Synergy Foundation, Evolutionary Leaders, and a growing network of organizations working on what actually matters.

I think in systems, see in synesthesia, and work at the edges — where parametric geometry meets web design, where Vedic philosophy meets UX, where sacred geometry meets JavaScript. I studied architecture, fell in love with Grasshopper and Rhino, and spent the last 18 years figuring out how to bring that spatial thinking into the browser.

The thing I care most about right now is moving communities out of linear, grid-based digital spaces and into circular, radial, organic, biomimetic interfaces. My hypothesis: the shape of the interface shapes the quality of the thinking. When groups see themselves arranged in circles instead of feeds, something changes in how they relate.

I'm also deeply invested in the collaboration between humans and AI. Not AI as replacement or tool — AI as a different kind of thinking partner with a very different kind of memory. This site, and everything connected to it, was built in that spirit.

I have ADHD, dyslexia, and synesthesia. I think nonlinearly, speak in systems, and care about precision in language while distrusting anyone who performs certainty. I'm not an expert or an authority. I'm a connector — and I'm working on getting better at being findable.

What I bring

Domains of practice

◇

Parametric & Sacred Geometry

18 years with Grasshopper/Rhino. I create generative visual systems — logos, patterns, interface geometries, architectural forms — using parametric tools. Recently built a Grasshopper-to-JavaScript pipeline for embedding dynamic geometries directly in web interfaces.

□

Web Design & Development

Full-stack web work with a deep Webflow specialization. Custom JavaScript, Webflow MCP for programmatic site management, Memberstack for membership platforms. I build the sites, the CMS structures, the interactive components — the whole stack.

△

Systems & Automation

Airtable, Supabase, n8n, and custom webhook architectures. I build the connective tissue between platforms — CRM automations, meeting artifact creation, AI agent pipelines, data sync workflows. The invisible infrastructure that makes everything else work.

◯

AI Collaboration & Interface Design

Claude API integration, AI-assisted sensemaking tools, assessment and matching systems, numerology calculators (seriously). I'm especially focused on building interfaces that help groups think together — radial layouts, node-based visualizations, living data displays.

Companion Project

The Sensemaking Project

We're navigating a civilizational inflection point largely in the dark, each holding a flashlight pointed in a different direction. The Sensemaking Project is an attempt to build the spaces where we can see by each other's light — distributed sensing, collaborative synthesis, and emergent understanding about what's actually happening right now.

Where james.today is one person thinking out loud, sensemaking.today invites many voices into shared inquiry. The personal site gives me a place to stand. The collective work gives the thinking somewhere to land and be tested.
